Detailed Manners for Students

  • Respect Others: Always treat your classmates, teachers, and others with respect. Use polite language and listen carefully when others speak.
  • Be Punctual: Arrive on time for your classes and other commitments. It shows responsibility and respect for others' time.
  • Show Gratitude: Thank teachers, fellow students, and staff when they help you or offer assistance. A simple "Thank you" goes a long way.
  • Work Hard: Dedicate yourself to your studies. Completing assignments on time and putting effort into your work shows your commitment to learning.
  • Be Honest: Always tell the truth. Avoid cheating or misleading others. Integrity is crucial in academic settings.
  • Stay Organized: Keep your materials, assignments, and study areas neat. It helps you stay focused and productive.
  • Practice Good Table Manners: If you're in a cafeteria or a dining setting, chew with your mouth closed, don't speak with food in your mouth, and clean up after yourself.
