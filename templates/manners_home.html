Manners for Students

  • Always say "Please" and "Thank You."
  • Respect your teachers, elders, and classmates.
  • Listen attentively when someone is speaking.
  • Keep your surroundings clean and organized.
  • Use polite language and avoid rude words.
  • Be punctual and value others' time.
  • Help others in need and show kindness.
  • Maintain good hygiene and dress neatly.
  • Apologize sincerely when making a mistake.
  • Follow rules and respect everyone's space.
