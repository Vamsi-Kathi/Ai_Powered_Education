Health & Wellbeing Tips for Students

1. Maintain a Balanced Diet

Eat nutritious meals with proteins, vegetables, and whole grains to keep your energy levels high.

2. Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to stay focused and refreshed.

3. Get Enough Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep every night to improve memory and concentration.

4. Take Breaks While Studying

Use the Pomodoro technique: study for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break.

5. Exercise Regularly

Engage in physical activities like walking, stretching, or yoga to reduce stress.

6. Practice Good Posture

Sit upright while studying to prevent back pain and improve concentration.

7. Limit Screen Time

Avoid excessive phone or laptop usage, and take regular eye breaks.

8. Manage Stress Effectively

Practice meditation, deep breathing, or listen to music to stay relaxed.

9. Maintain Social Connections

Interact with friends and family to maintain a positive mindset.

10. Avoid Caffeine & Junk Food

Limit caffeine and junk food intake to avoid energy crashes.
